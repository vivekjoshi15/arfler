I am Harry Mokm. I live in Seattle, Washington, and grew up in Vietnam. As a child, I witnessed the violence and destruction caused by war, which produced a strong desire within me to help to achieve a better world. As a matter of fact, I have dreamt of doing so since I first set foot on North American soil over four decades ago. A better world seems far away to us today, but I believe we can get there if we understand the nature of our problems and have a plan to solve them.

Today, I have reached the age of 70. Instead of retiring and withdrawing from the world, I decided to create several new ventures to make my dream a reality. I started writing and publishing books that I hope will help create positive change. At the same time, I am asking readers like you to purchase my books to help accumulate the seed funding for my start-ups: a healthy food service and a socially cooperative rewards network, each of which works in an interlocking manner to inspire people toward a healthier and more productive life.

I hope you enjoy my books and purchase them for yourself, for family members, and for friends. The proceeds from all of these books will go toward my projects to build a better tomorrow for all of us.

After reading my book(s), if you like them, please share my website mokm.com with your friends, relatives and colleagues so that more and more people will come to support these ideas. We are building the power to make it happen, the “better world” we have all been dreaming of.

If you would like to receive information and updates from me about these projects, please sign up on MOKM.com and I will be happy to share with you about them.

I think you will enjoy my stories, and by purchasing them, you are making a contribution to projects that will benefit the environment, our children, and society as a whole.

Harry Mokm
Seattle, Washington
